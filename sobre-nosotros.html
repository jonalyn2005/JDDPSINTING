Our Story

JDD PAINTING was born in 2009 with a clear vision: to revolutionize the painting industry by combining traditional professional techniques with cutting-edge eco-friendly materials in Oklahoma.

Founded by Jonathan Moreno, a professional with over 20 years of experience in the sector, our company began as a small family business with a big dream: to create beautiful and healthy spaces without compromising the environment.

Throughout these years, we have completed more than 500 projects across Oklahoma, from family residences to large commercial complexes, always maintaining our commitment to excellence and sustainability.

2009

JDD PAINTING Founded

Jonathan Moreno establishes JDD PAINTING in Oklahoma City with a focus on eco-friendly painting solutions and exceptional customer service.

2012

Eco-Certification Achievement

Became the first painting company in Oklahoma to achieve Green Seal certification for eco-friendly practices and VOC-free materials.

2015

Commercial Expansion

Expanded services to include large commercial projects, partnering with major businesses across Oklahoma and establishing our reputation in the commercial sector.

2018

Technology Integration

Implemented advanced color-matching technology and digital project management systems, improving efficiency and customer experience.

2020

COVID-19 Adaptation

Successfully adapted operations during the pandemic with enhanced safety protocols and contactless service options, maintaining our commitment to Oklahoma families.

2022

500+ Projects Milestone

Reached the milestone of 500 completed projects across Oklahoma, from Tulsa to Norman, establishing JDD PAINTING as a trusted local leader.

2024

Industry Leadership

Recognized as Oklahoma's premier eco-friendly painting service, continuing to innovate with sustainable practices and cutting-edge techniques.
