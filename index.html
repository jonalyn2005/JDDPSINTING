Frequently Asked Questions

We answer the most common questions about our professional painting services in Oklahoma.

How long does it take to paint a complete house?

The time depends on the property size and type of work. An average 1,600 sq ft house takes between 5-7 working days. This includes surface preparation, primer, and two finish coats.

What type of paints do you use?

We exclusively use certified eco-friendly paints with low VOC content. We work with recognized brands like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and Behr, prioritizing environmentally friendly products.

Do you include paint in the estimate?

Yes, our estimates include all materials: paints, primers, tools and protective materials. There are no hidden costs, the final price is agreed upon from the start.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

We offer a 5-year warranty on interior painting and 7 years on exterior painting. The warranty covers application defects and problems related to the quality of our work, not normal wear from use.

Do you work on weekends?

Yes, we offer weekend services for clients who require scheduling flexibility. For commercial projects, we also work night hours to minimize business disruption.

How do I request a quote?

You can request your free quote through our contact form, by phone or WhatsApp. We conduct a free site visit to evaluate your project and deliver a detailed quote within 24-48 hours.
